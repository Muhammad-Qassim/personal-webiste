Intro

I absolutely love learning about new technology and how they work, and I hope to one day use my knowledge to help others in some way. Outside of school, I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, going on hikes, and playing video games.

I really enjoy coding and am proficient in several languages, including C++, Python, Go, HTML and CSS. I find it to be a very rewarding experience when I can take a complex problem and break it down into manageable pieces of code that work together to solve the issue. It's also gratifying to see the end result of my efforts when everything comes together and works as intended.

I am currently engaged in the development of a Movie Inventory Management System using Java and MySQL, incorporating robust CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionalities and a versatile Content Management System (CMS). This project involves creating a sophisticated database system to efficiently manage movie data, allowing for the seamless addition, retrieval, modification, and removal of movie records. Additionally, the integration of a CMS will provide a user-friendly interface for content management, making it easier to update and maintain the movie inventory.
